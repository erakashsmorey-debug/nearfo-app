Our Commitment to Protecting Minors from Sexual Abuse and Exploitation

Our Commitment to Child Safety

At Nearfo, the safety and well-being of children on our platform is our highest priority. We are committed to maintaining a secure environment for all users, with particular focus on protecting minors from harm, exploitation, and abuse.

We recognize the serious threat posed by Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and have implemented comprehensive measures throughout our platform to detect, prevent, and report such content and behavior.

Zero Tolerance Policy: Nearfo has a zero-tolerance policy toward any content or behavior that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers children. Any violations result in immediate action, including account suspension, content removal, and reporting to law enforcement when appropriate.

Zero Tolerance for CSAE

Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) includes:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM), including images and videos of minors in sexual situations
  • Grooming and solicitation of minors for sexual purposes
  • Sexual exploitation through coercion or manipulation
  • Online enticement of children for sexual contact
  • Distribution of obscene materials to minors
  • Any other content or conduct that endangers child safety

Nearfo condemns these activities in their entirety. We work diligently to identify and remove such content and suspend users responsible for such behavior.

How Nearfo Prevents CSAE Content

🤖 AI-Powered Moderation

We deploy advanced machine learning algorithms to automatically detect and flag potentially illegal content, including:

  • Photographic and video content depicting child sexual abuse
  • Suspicious behavioral patterns indicative of grooming
  • Age-inappropriate communications between users
  • Known illegal content databases and hash matching

👥 Human Review Teams

Our trained safety specialists review flagged content and user reports to make final determinations about policy violations. Our human teams ensure that context is properly considered while maintaining vigilance against CSAE.

📢 User Reporting Mechanisms

We provide accessible in-app reporting tools that allow users to quickly report suspicious activity, inappropriate content, or potential exploitation. All reports are prioritized and investigated by our safety team.

🔍 Content Scanning

All user-uploaded images and videos are scanned against known databases of illegal content. We use industry-leading technologies to identify and prevent the distribution of CSAM.

📊 Behavioral Analysis

Our systems monitor user behavior patterns for signs of predatory activity, including unusual messaging, rapid user age targeting, and other indicators of grooming behavior.

How Users Can Report Concerns

In-App Reporting

Users can report concerning content or behavior directly through the Nearfo app:

  • Use the report button on any post, profile, or message
  • Select the relevant violation category
  • Provide additional context or description
  • Submit for immediate review by our safety team

Cooperation with Law Enforcement

Nearfo actively cooperates with law enforcement agencies, child safety organizations, and other platforms to combat child exploitation. Our commitments include:

  • Swift Reporting: We promptly report suspected CSAM and child exploitation to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and appropriate law enforcement authorities
  • Evidence Preservation: We maintain detailed logs and evidence of violations for law enforcement investigations
  • Information Sharing: We collaborate with other platforms and organizations to identify and track bad actors across the internet
  • Legal Compliance: We comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding child protection, including FOSTA-SESTA and international CSAM laws
  • Law Enforcement Support: We provide legal assistance and information to law enforcement investigating crimes against children

Age Verification Measures

Nearfo implements age verification and age-appropriate protections:

  • Account Creation Requirements: All users must be at least 13 years old to create an account, with age verification during registration
  • Age-Based Access Controls: Certain features and content are restricted based on user age
  • Parental Controls: For users under 18, parents and guardians have tools to monitor activity and set safety preferences
  • Privacy Protections: Minor accounts have enhanced privacy protections, including restricted public visibility
  • Messaging Restrictions: Messaging from unknown users to minors includes additional verification steps
  • Educational Content: We provide resources to help younger users understand online safety and recognize risky behavior
