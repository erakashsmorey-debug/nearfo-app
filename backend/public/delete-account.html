Delete Your Account

Last updated: March 21, 2026

At Nearfo, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your Nearfo account and all associated data, you can do so by following the steps below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Nearfo app on your device.
  2. Go to Profile by tapping the profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.
  3. Tap the Settings (gear icon) in the top right corner.
  4. Scroll down and tap Account Security.
  5. Tap Delete Account.
  6. Confirm your identity by entering your phone number or OTP.
  7. Tap Permanently Delete to confirm.
Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will not be able to recover any of your data.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days:

  • Profile information — name, phone number, email, bio, date of birth, and avatar
  • Messages — all chat messages, including text, photos, videos, and voice recordings
  • Media files — all photos, videos, and voice recordings you uploaded
  • Location data — all stored GPS coordinates and location history
  • Posts and stories — all content you shared on the platform
  • Connections — your friend list and connection requests
  • Device tokens — push notification tokens associated with your account
  • User ID — your unique account identifier

Data Retention

After you request deletion, your account will be deactivated immediately and your profile will no longer be visible to other users. All personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days of the deletion request.

Some data may be retained for up to 90 days in encrypted backups before being fully purged. We may also retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you.
