Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Nearfo ("we", "us", "our") operates the Nearfo mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Nearfo:

Account Information: Phone number (for verification), name, username, email (optional), profile photo, date of birth (optional), bio.

Content: Posts, stories, reels, comments, messages, and media you upload.

Location Data: With your permission, we collect your approximate location to show nearby content and users. You can disable this in your device settings.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including features used, content viewed, and engagement patterns.

Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and network information.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide and improve the Nearfo service, show relevant local content, connect you with nearby users, send notifications, ensure safety and security, and comply with legal obligations.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Amazon Web Services (AWS) servers. We encrypt sensitive information including phone numbers, messages, and personal details. Media files are stored on AWS S3 with CloudFront CDN for fast delivery.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with: service providers (AWS, Firebase, MongoDB Atlas) who help us operate the app, law enforcement when required by law, and other users based on your privacy settings (public/local/private profile).

Your Rights

You can: access, update, or delete your account and data at any time through Settings. Request a copy of your data by contacting us. Opt out of location tracking in device settings.

Children's Privacy

Nearfo is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Copyright (DMCA)

If you believe content on Nearfo infringes your copyright, you can submit a takedown request through the app (Settings > Report Copyright Infringement) or contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
