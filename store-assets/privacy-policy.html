Nearfo Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

Nearfo ("we", "our", "us") operates the Nearfo mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Phone Number: We use your phone number for account verification via Firebase Authentication. Your phone number is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

Location Data: We collect your approximate location to show you nearby content and users. Your exact coordinates are never displayed to other users — only your city/state is shown.

Profile Information: Name, username, bio, and profile photo that you voluntarily provide.

Content: Posts, messages, and comments you create within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide and improve our service, including showing you relevant local content, connecting you with nearby users, and delivering notifications.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas (cloud database) and AWS S3 (media files). All data transfer is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at er.akashsmorey@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at er.akashsmorey@gmail.com.
