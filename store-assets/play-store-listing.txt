APP NAME: Nearfo - Know Your Circle

SHORT DESCRIPTION (80 chars max):
Connect with people nearby. Share vibes, chat live, and discover your circle.

FULL DESCRIPTION:

Nearfo is a location-first social platform that connects you with the people around you. Whether you're exploring a new city or getting to know your neighborhood, Nearfo brings your local community to life.

KEY FEATURES:

Local Feed — See what people near you are sharing. 80% local content, 20% global vibes for the perfect mix.

Discover Nearby — Find people, trending topics, and interesting conversations happening within 200km of you.

Live Chat — Real-time messaging with typing indicators and online status. Connect instantly with people in your circle.

Post Your Vibes — Share text posts with mood emojis and photo attachments. Express yourself authentically.

Nearfo Score — Build your reputation in your local community. The more you engage, the higher your score.

Interactive Map — See who's around you on a beautiful interactive map. Find new connections based on proximity.

Privacy First — Your exact location is never shared. We only use approximate areas to connect you with nearby people.
